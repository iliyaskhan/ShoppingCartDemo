Shopping Cart Demo

A simple shopping cart demo using Backbone.js and jQuery-UI. The idea is to have basic shopping cart behaviors such as:

  • selecting products based on some categories,
  • drag and drop it to the basket
  • removing selected item from basket
  • reset basket
  • display total price

Live demo can be found here : http://www.spicevanjava.nl/shoppingCartDemo
